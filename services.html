RPA Technology Selection

APAROK helps you selecting the right Robotic Process Automation (RPA) technology that involves careful consideration of various factors to ensure it aligns with your organization's needs, goals, and technical requirements.

  • Requirements: Identify the specific business processes you want to automate and determine factors such as process complexity, volume, frequency, scalability, regulatory compliance, and integration with existing systems.
  • RPA Vendors: We will research different RPA vendors such as UiPath, Blue Prism, Automation Anywhere, Power Automate and evaluate their features, capabilities, pricing models, deployment options (cloud-based or on-premises).
  • Technical Capabilities: Evaluate the technical capabilities, including process discovery, bot analytics, scalability, security features, integration capabilities, and support for various automation technologies (e.g., OCR, AI/ML).
  • Total Cost of Ownership (TCO): Evaluate the total cost of ownership, including upfront licensing fees, implementation costs, maintenance fees, and ongoing support costs.
  • Stakeholders: Involve key stakeholders, obtain buy-in from decision-makers and secure budget approval before finalizing the selection of an RPA technology.

Process Discovery & Automation

APAROK powers in process discovery of identifying and documenting business processes of your enterprise. It involves systematically gathering information about how tasks and activities are currently performed, understanding the flow of work, and analyzing the underlying steps and decision points.

  • Process Identification: The first step is to identify the processes within the organization that are suitable for automation. We achieve this by conducting interviews with stakeholders, analyzing existing documentation, and observing current workflows.
  • Process Mining: We offer a data-driven approach to analyze existing processes using event logs or other data sources. It involves extracting insights from these data sources to understand how processes are actually being executed, identify deviations from the intended process flow, and uncover opportunities for automation.
  • Process Design: Design and develop the automated workflows based on the documented processes and automation requirements. This involves creating workflow diagrams, defining rules and conditions, configuring automation scripts or bots, and integrating with existing systems or applications.
  • Documentation & Analysis: We help mapping out the steps involved, identifying inputs and outputs, documenting decision points, and capturing any variations or exceptions. Creating Process Definition Document (PDD) involves examining these documented processes to identify inefficiencies, bottlenecks, or areas for improvement.
  • Automation Potential Assessment: We analyze the processes, assess the automation potential of each process. Determine which processes are repetitive, rule-based, and have a high volume of transactions, as these are typically good candidates for automation.

RPA Implementation & Deployment

APAROK helps in implementing and deploying Robotic Process Automation (RPA) involves several key steps to ensure successful integration and realization of automation benefits organizations can effectively implement and deploy RPA solutions to streamline processes, improve efficiency, and drive business growth.

  • Design Workflows: We define the detailed steps involved in automated workflows. Design the logic, decision points, error handling mechanisms, and expection handling procedures using RE frameworks and other best parterns and practices.
  • RPA Bot Development Creating Solution Design Document (SDD) which delineates architecture & data flows. The bot development process involves developing and configuring bots to execute automated workflow. Depending on requirements/processes we deploy both attended and un-attended bots (on-premises/hybrid/cloud).
  • Deployment Planning : We develop a deployment plan outlining the steps and timeline for rolling out the RPA solution into production. Coordinate with relevant stakeholders to schedule deployment activities and ensure minimal disruption to ongoing operations.
  • Deployment Execution: We Deploy the RPA bots into the production environment according to the deployment plan. Monitor the deployment process closely to address any issues or challenges that may arise during the transition to automation.
  • Monitor & Optimize: We establish monitoring mechanisms to track the performance and effectiveness of the deployed RPA bots. Collect data on key performance indicators (KPIs) such as process cycle time, error rates, and cost savings. Use performance data to identify opportunities for optimization and refinement of the RPA implementation.
